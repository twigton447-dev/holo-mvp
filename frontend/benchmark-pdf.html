Why this matters

The expensive failures happen at the threshold.

Most benchmarks measure what models know. Holo Benchmark measures where model judgment stops being safe enough to act on. That matters when an agent is about to move money, grant access, or execute a contract — because the cost of a miss is no longer a bad answer. It is a real-world action.

What this benchmark is
Not a capability leaderboard
Not ranking models by general intelligence. Measuring failure at the action boundary.
Built for reproducibility
The harness, scenarios, and methodology are open so validators can run the benchmark themselves.
Focused on irreversible actions
Designed around high-consequence workflows where a wrong approval becomes expensive immediately.
