The Blindspot Atlas is an interactive visualization of Holo's benchmark results, including model-by-model turn reasoning, verdict trajectories, and failure pattern analysis across scenarios and domains.

Access Notice

The interactive Atlas contains proprietary trace data — model reasoning chains, adversarial turn sequences, and failure patterns across frontier model families. This material is available to qualified technical reviewers and design partners under NDA.

The public benchmark summary, scenario payloads, and high-level methodology are available at holoengine.ai/benchmark. Published scenario payloads are available in the payloads directory.

Public benchmark materials are intentionally limited to preserve the integrity of the evaluation harness and protect proprietary adjudication mechanics. Full traces, payloads, and reproducibility materials are available to qualified technical reviewers under NDA.
